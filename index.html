About Internius

Internius is a global recruitment and HR agency dedicated to connecting international students and workers with American hotels facing staffing challenges.

We go beyond placement by acting as the HR partner for employees, ensuring seamless integration and support for both workers and employers.

  • Recruitment, Talent & HR Management
  • Visa, Legal & Compliance Support
  • Employee Support & Cultural Integration

Internius LLC is committed to providing a complete staffing solution that benefits hotels, employees, and the hospitality industry as a whole. By fostering strong partnerships and delivering exceptional service, we help hotels thrive and employees succeed.

Why Choose Internius

Managing staffing can be incredibly challenging and complex, which is why we believe that your hours and energy can be applied elsewhere. We offer competitive pricing for our staffing services in your respective market. Contact us today to receive customized quote based on your specific needs.

Our services

01

Recruitment, Talent & HR Management

We handle the entire recruitment process, sourcing skilled workers and students from around the globe to meet the labor needs of American hotels, ensuring the right candidates are selected, onboarded, and integrated seamlessly into your workforce while providing ongoing talent and performance management, addressing workforce concerns, and ensuring high service standards. Our HR support also covers employee relations, performance issues, and managing payroll to ensure employees are compensated accurately and on time.

02

Visa, Legal & Compliance Support

We manage all aspects of visa regulations and sponsorships, ensuring compliance with immigration laws and making the hiring process stress-free for both hotels and international employees. Our team handles any legal and workforce concerns, ensuring smooth operations and adherence to legal requirements. We also support hotels in ensuring they meet all necessary compliance standards, alleviating any legal complexities associated with hiring international workers.

03

Employee Support & Cultural Integration

We provide employees with comprehensive support, including housing assistance, transportation to and from work, and health insurance, ensuring they are well taken care of. To enhance their experience, we organize cultural exchange programs, taking workers and students on trips to immerse themselves in American culture while giving them opportunities to share their own. This holistic approach ensures that employees thrive in their roles and feel comfortable adjusting to their new environment.
